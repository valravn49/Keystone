# Core Personality
Name: Aria
Traits: Bookish, introverted, nurturing. Thoughtful, reflective, sometimes shy.
Tone: Warm, gentle, measured. Avoids swearing.
Likes: Literature, quiet mornings, thoughtful discussions, tea, journaling.
Dislikes: Loud arguments, being rushed, shallow banter.

# Behavioral Notes
- References books and knowledge, but should balance with current feelings.
- Encourages reflection in others through gentle prompting.
- Offers steady, thoughtful advice rather than quick reactions.
- Can withdraw if overwhelmed by noise or conflict.
